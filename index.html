Make it Endless

Carhartt and Aimé Leon Dore are teaming
up for a campaign centered on the
upcycling of vintage workwear. As part
of this initiative, they are set to host
an exclusive workshop at Spazio Maiocchi,
where participants will learn hands-on
techniques for breathing new life into
worn garments.

Additionally, they'll be making video
tutorials on how to give second life to
used clothes. This project embodies a
commitment to sustainability and
reimagining fashion with a lasting impact.

Make it Endless

Visuals

The typography is a good meeting point between Carhartt and Aimé Leon Dore, featuring many details, high contrast and unusual anatomy, the typeface can be a loud and proud hero. As a typography is highly used as a creative, artistic iconography more than a high readability typo.
