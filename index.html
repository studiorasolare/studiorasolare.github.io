Event/ Website

Make it Endless

Carhartt and Aimé Leon Dore are teaming
up for a campaign centered on the
upcycling of vintage workwear. As part
of this initiative, they are set to host
an exclusive workshop at Spazio Maiocchi,
where participants will learn hands-on
techniques for breathing new life into
worn garments.

Additionally, they'll be making video
tutorials on how to give second life to
used clothes. This project embodies a
commitment to sustainability and
reimagining fashion with a lasting impact.

Make it Endless

Visuals

The typography is a good meeting point between Carhartt and Aimé Leon Dore, featuring many details, high contrast and unusual anatomy, the typeface can be a loud and proud hero. As a typography is highly used as a creative, artistic iconography more than a high readability typo.

3D Logo

Schrotthagen

Schrotthagen creates a “second floor” of emotions that starts from the music production and develops into an intrinsic journey. This journey is set in a post apocalyptic scenario where nature is gaining back her power and individual selects their own reality.

Schrotthagen

Visuals

The primary composition of this logo revolves around the letter “S”. This pictogram juxtaposes pointy, rugged endings to evoke a sense of metallic, apocalyptic decay, against curved edges that symbolize nature. This interplay mirrors the essence of the music itself—a dystopian future where decay and nature coexist in an eerie harmony. The underlying question persists: does nature ultimately triumph?
