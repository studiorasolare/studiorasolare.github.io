Milan

Why was SOS House created?

We started as a creative studio, but SOS House emerged from a personal need. Milan can feel lonely, and we wanted to create a space where people could connect without judgment. Our goal is to create a sense of belonging for creative people, allowing them to interact without the pressure to perform. We want you to simply be yourself.
